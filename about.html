Bert Sie

Managing Director

Bert co-developed the “Double U” concept with Teresa in early 2022, focusing on go-to-market and growth strategies for technology-driven companies. He currently serves as Head of Sales & Business Development for Beammwave and AlixLabs in Taiwan.

Bert began his career as a Sales Engineer at EDOM, a top-7 global IC distributor, and later advanced into senior leadership roles, founding the company’s first mass-market business group and leading sales and marketing initiatives. Earlier in his career, he managed product teams across multiple applications and supported five startups from 0 to 1.

He holds an MBA from National Central University, Taiwan.
